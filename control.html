On Control

As Christianity is so intimately linked with the history of Africa, it traces its modern roots to European colonialism, which early 19th century Christian missionaries found themselves pre-empting. Christianity was a valuable tool for cultural hegemony, and from countries where the state and church overlap, a tool of control too.
